01 — 在时代的交汇处诞生

Born Where Change Begins

当AlphaFold解开蛋白质折叠之谜，当GPT重新定义人机交互边界，当量子计算从实验室走向产业化——我们正站在一个由人工智能驱动的全新时代起点。

剑桥大学，这座拥有八世纪历史的学术殿堂，不仅是科学革命的摇篮——从这里走出了牛顿的经典力学、达尔文的进化论、麦克斯韦的电磁场理论、图灵的计算机、霍金的时间简史——更是当代科技创新的重要策源地。

在康河畔的古老学院与现代实验室之间，华人学者与创业者的身影日渐显著。Cambridge CEO 正是在这样的历史契机与创新土壤中孕育而生——一个使命驱动的创新共同体。

汇聚剑桥及全球华人创业智慧，搭建连接学术前沿与产业实践、东方市场与西方技术的多维生态。

01 — Born Where Change Begins

Our Story

As AlphaFold unravels the mysteries of protein folding, GPT redefines human-machine interaction, and quantum computing moves from laboratory to industry — we stand at the dawn of a new era driven by artificial intelligence.

The University of Cambridge, an eight-century-old cradle of scientific revolution — home to Newton's mechanics, Darwin's evolution, Maxwell's electromagnetism, Turing's computation, and Hawking's cosmology — remains a vital source of contemporary innovation.

Along the banks of the River Cam, between ancient colleges and modern laboratories, Chinese scholars and entrepreneurs are playing an increasingly prominent role. Cambridge CEO was born from this convergence — a mission-driven community of innovators.

Uniting the entrepreneurial wisdom of Cambridge and Chinese innovators worldwide — bridging academic frontiers with industry, Eastern markets with Western technology.

04 — Unique Value Proposition

What Sets Us Apart

01

纯正的剑桥基因

与剑桥大学创业加速器、各学院技术研究中心及全球校友会深度合作，第一时间接触最前沿的创新成果。

02

跨文化的深度理解

核心团队与顾问委员会成员大多拥有在东西方学习、工作和创业的亲身经历，理解语言背后的思维模式与商业逻辑。

03

代际融合的社区

覆盖20岁到70岁的创新者——从《自然》论文作者到B轮融资创始人，独特的"传帮带"文化让智慧持续流动。

04

开创精神的践行者

不满足于跟随趋势，而是从剑桥实验室出发，孵化颠覆性创意，创造跨境创新的新范式，以原创力量塑造未来。

01

Authentic Cambridge DNA

Deep partnerships with Cambridge's accelerators, college research centres, and global alumni networks give us first access to cutting-edge innovation.

02

Deep Cross-Cultural Understanding

Our core team have lived, studied, and built companies across East and West — we understand the mindsets and business logic on both sides.

03

A Multigenerational Community

From PhD students publishing in Nature to serial entrepreneurs planning exits — our mentorship culture keeps wisdom flowing across generations.

04

Pioneers, Not Followers

We don't follow trends — we originate from Cambridge's laboratories, incubating disruptive ideas and creating new models for cross-border innovation that shape the future.
