关于中国行

Cambridge CEO（Cambridge Chinese Entrepreneur Organisation 剑桥大学全球华人创业者协会）是剑桥大学唯一注册的官方华人创业者协会，致力于帮助剑桥大学及英国知名院校的学生和校友链接中国的科技创新创业所需的创投基金、产业资源、专家导师与供应链。

China Trek 创业中国行是协会每年举办的旗舰活动。我们将带领来自剑桥、牛津及伦敦等高校的学生及校友创业家，走进中国最活跃的科技创新城市——理解一条正在成形的产业链：机器人如何被制造出来，人工智能如何进入物理世界，AI通用模型如何走进商业及人类生活，资本如何判断未来，创业者如何在时代变化中找到自己的位置。

About China Trek

Cambridge CEO (Cambridge Chinese Entrepreneur Organisation) is the only officially registered Chinese entrepreneurship society at the University of Cambridge, dedicated to connecting students and alumni from Cambridge and leading UK institutions with China's venture capital, industrial resources, expert mentors, and supply chains.

China Trek is the society's annual flagship programme. We lead student and alumni entrepreneurs from Cambridge, Oxford, and London universities into China's most dynamic innovation cities — to understand an emerging industrial chain: how robots are built, how AI enters the physical world, how foundation models reshape business and daily life, how capital evaluates the future, and how entrepreneurs find their place in a changing era.
